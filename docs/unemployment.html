Industry Impact



With the shutdown of major operations around the world and sluggish sales due to lockdown, the industries started laying off employees to avoid margin leakage or stay afloat. In the unemployment graph, countries are segregated based on the effect of unemployment.

  • Countries like Fiji, Norway are facing the worst impact of Covid-19 and have maximum increase in unemployment since last year.
  • Countries like United States, South Africa are also experiencing a major downturn in economy and showing a huge increase in unemployment rate.
  • Countries in segment moderately high and moderate are developing economies that are experiencing the COVID19 effects gradually.
